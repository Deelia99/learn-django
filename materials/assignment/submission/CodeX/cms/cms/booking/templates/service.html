Welcome to CodeX Car Maintenance Services. These are the services we offer.

Oil Change and Oil Filter

Regularly replacing the engine oil and oil filter to keep the engine lubricated and running smoothly.

Windshield Wipers and Fluid

Replacing worn-out windshield wipers and ensuring an adequate supply of windshield washer fluid for clear visibility during rain or snow.

Air and Cabin Filter Replacement

Swapping out the air filter to maintain clean airflow to the engine and replacing the cabin filter for fresh and clean air inside the vehicle.

Tire Replacement and Wheel Balance and Rotation

Changing worn-out tires, ensuring proper wheel balance, and rotating tires to promote even wear and prolong their lifespan.

Battery Replacement

Installing a new battery to ensure reliable starting and electrical power for the vehicle.

Brake Repair

Addressing issues with the braking system, including brake pad replacement, rotor resurfacing or replacement, and brake fluid flush to maintain safe and efficient braking performance.

Coolant System Services

Performing maintenance tasks such as coolant flush and refill to prevent engine overheating and ensure proper cooling system function.
